About Our Face Detection System

This system is designed to provide a secure, automated way to register and manage student attendance using facial recognition technology. Built with Python, OpenCV, and Django, it captures faces via a webcam, detects and recognizes them, and stores attendance data securely.

Our goal is to eliminate manual attendance processes, prevent proxy attendance, and enhance school or institutional security. The system supports both students and teachers, offering features like registration, login, real-time face detection, and attendance viewing.

This project is part of an academic initiative to explore the real-world application of computer vision in education and security environments.
